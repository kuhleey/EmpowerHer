Our Story

Founded in 2025, EmpowerHer is dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology through education, mentorship, and community building.

Our Mission

Empowering Women in the Tech Industry

We believe diversity drives innovation. Our mission is to create opportunities for women at all stages of their tech careers, from students to executives.

Through our programs, we provide the skills, network, and confidence women need to thrive in technology fields.

Join us in building a future where women are equally represented at all levels of the technology sector.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind EmpowerHer's mission

Founder & CEO

Kuhle Mbono

Student

Kuhle Mbono is a software development student and the founder of this platform. Her interest in tech began after completing short online courses, which helped her discover a passion for programming and led her to pursue formal studies in software development.

Through her own learning journey, she noticed how difficult it can be for beginners to know where to start and stay motivated. This inspired her to create a platform that simplifies entry into tech for women by providing curated free resources, guidance, and learning paths.The goal of the platform is to make tech more accessible and help women explore whether it is a path they want to pursue, at their own pace.

"Women are still underrepresented in tech. This platform exists to create a starting point where women can explore, learn, and see what’s possible for them in the tech space." - Kuhle Mbono

Empowering women to explore, learn, and thrive in technology one step at a time.
