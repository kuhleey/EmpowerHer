Our Story

Founded in 2025, EmpowerHer is dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology through education, mentorship, and community building.

Our Mission

Empowering women at all stages of their tech careers

Empowering Women in the Tech Industry

We believe diversity drives innovation. Our mission is to create opportunities for women at all stages of their tech careers, from students to executives.

Through our programs, we provide the skills, network, and confidence women need to thrive in technology fields. We partner with leading tech companies to create more inclusive workplaces.

Join us in building a future where women are equally represented at all levels of the technology sector.

Skills Breakdown

Explore tech career paths and find where your passion fits.

UX/UI Designer

Designs how apps and websites look and feel. You focus on making digital experiences intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing for users.

Figma Wireframing User research

Product Designer

Bridges design and business strategy. You shape entire products from concept to launch, balancing user needs with business goals.

Prototyping Design systems Strategy

Software Developer

Builds the apps, websites, and systems people use every day. You write code to bring ideas to life — from simple websites to complex platforms.

HTML/CSS JavaScript Java

Cybersecurity Specialist

Protects systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. You think like a hacker to defend organisations against threats and vulnerabilities.

Ethical hacking Networking Risk management

Data Scientist

Turns raw data into meaningful insights. You analyse patterns and trends to help businesses make smarter, evidence-based decisions.

Python SQL Statistics

AI/ML Engineer

Builds systems that learn and make decisions on their own. You work on the technology behind recommendation engines, chatbots, and intelligent automation.

Machine learning Python Neural networks

IT Support Specialist

Keeps technology running smoothly for people and organisations. You troubleshoot hardware and software issues, helping users solve technical problems day to day.

Troubleshooting Networking Customer support
